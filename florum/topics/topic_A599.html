President Hussein receives congratulatory telegrams

Baghdad, Oct. 17, INA

President Saddam Hussein has received a congratulatory telegram from Vice-Chairman of Revolution Command Council Mr. Izzat Ibrahim.

Mr. Ibrahim congratulated President Hussein on winning 100% of votes for the Presidency post, which represents a big hit to the U.S and British governments.

In this occasion, Iraqis have showed their absolute love to President Hussein. In this referendum, Iraqis unmasked the false claims uttered by the US evil administration against the relationship between the President and the Iraqi people, Mr. Ibrahim said.

President Hussein also received a congratulatory telegram from the gathering parties in the National, Patriotic and Progressive Front on this occasion.

Secretary-General of the Front Mr. Abdul-Baki Abdul-Khreem Al-Sadoun said that this occasion represents a serious letter to Bush and Blair that People in Iraq are with their President and they love him.

Mr. Al-Sadoun expressed pledge to remain loyal to the President and defend Iraqs sovereignty and security.

The President also received a telegram from Jordanian parties and popular forces, which are currently visiting Baghdad.

The telegram confirmed that the referendum showed the world the real relation and love between Iraqi people and their leadership.

This democratic process is a clear answer to US-Zionists threats against Iraq, Arab nation and the Islamic states, the telegram said.
