Welcome to the Amstel Soccer Sixes - Sunday July 24th, 2005
The Amstel Soccer Sixes is a 6 against 6 men open age tournament played on Sunday July 24th on the 6 fields of soccer club RCH in Heemstede. The Amstel Soccer Sixes is an official part of the Regio Haarlem Cup tournament (Hollands biggest international soccer tournament).

Each of the 6 soccer fields will be divided into 3 reduced fields. Two small goals will be placed on each of the reduced fields. The tournament will start at 10 a.m. and will end at approximately 6 p.m. A total of 60 teams will be accepted in the Amstel Soccer Sixes.

This tournament is mainly a sociable event in which football and achievement (especially in the Elite Class) of course are important, but not as important as a pleasant day in a nice atmosphere over a drink and a snack.

The town of Heemstede is located only 15 minutes west of Amsterdam, the capital of the Netherlands. Every 10 minutes trains go from Haarlem central station to Amsterdam. Only 10 minutes east the sandy beaches of Zandvoort can be found. Come to the Amstel Soccer Sixes for a great soccer experience!
