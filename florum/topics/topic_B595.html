my fave sites
Alright boys, I'm here at the request of Monsieur van Beemen. As I'm currently between jobs I spend a fair proporotion of my time on English football websites...
Well my favourites are:

football.guardian.co.uk (v. good columnists)
soccernet.com
sport.telegraph.co.uk (registration required, but just do what I did & give them a fake email so they can't spam you)
www.redissue.co.uk (Man Utd only but very good, check out the forum, it's where I waste hours of my life...)
sport.independent.co.uk
check out the football section of www.newsnow.co.uk
www.uwsonline.com is ok for Man Utd stuff, ditto www.rednews.co.uk.

If you can read Spanish, marca.es is a must, and calciomercato.com for ALL the most improbable transfer gossip in Italian but written for 2 year-olds (eg did you know Michael Owen is joining Bolton? and other such crap).
www.football365.com is ok and occasionally funny but you do feel like you're reading 'the Sun'
